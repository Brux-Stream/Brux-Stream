Proxy with link aggregation

    A proxy with link aggregation is a powerful solution that combines multiple network links into a single, high-bandwidth connection, improving both performance and redundancy. For live streaming or video broadcasting, this means enhanced reliability, lower latency, and better utilization of available network resources, making it an ideal choice for demanding applications where uptime and performance are critical.

SRT Streaming protocol

    SRT (Secure Reliable Transport) is a cutting-edge video streaming protocol designed to deliver high-quality, low-latency streams over unpredictable networks. By optimizing packet recovery, encryption, and error correction,
    SRT ensures secure, reliable transmission even in challenging environments like public internet or mobile networks. Ideal for live broadcasting and remote production,
    SRT minimizes jitter and packet loss, enabling smooth, high-performance video delivery across long distances and varying network conditions.
